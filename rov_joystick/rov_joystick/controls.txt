------------------------------
Stick,Axis,Action,ROV Movement
------------------------------
Left Stick,UP,Surge (+),Moves Forward.

Left Stick,DOWN,Stop,Motors stop (cannot reverse).

Left Stick,LEFT,Yaw (+),Turns the nose to the Left.

Left Stick,RIGHT,Yaw (-),Turns the nose to the Right.

Right Stick,DOWN,Heave (-),Dives deeper.

Right Stick,UP,Surface,Stops vertical motors (ROV floats up).
